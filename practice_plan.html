Programme Philosophy
You already swing at scratch speed. The gap is consistency — tighter standard deviations across every metric. Each week builds on the last: establish fundamentals, then train under pressure, then transfer to the course.

Week 1
Foundations
13 May – 19 May
Goal: Establish consistent low point, feel neutral face at impact. No course play — pure drilling.
Wed
13
Range · 60 min
Low Point & Face Awareness
  • Feet together drill — 7 iron, 20 balls. Feel centred rotation, no slide.
  • Towel behind ball drill — 5 iron, 20 balls. Stop catching the towel.
  • Gate drill — driver, slow-motion, 15 reps. Feel face staying square.
  • Pump drill — driver, 10 reps. Check face angle at halfway through.
No free-hitting. Drills only today.

Short Game · 45 min
Wedge Clock Drill — Carry Mapping
  • PW: 10 balls at 9 o'clock, 10 at 10 o'clock, 10 full. Record avg carry for each.
  • Land on a 3-ft circle. Count how many out of 10 land inside — aim for 5+.
  • Finish: 10 full PW to a specific flag. Track lateral dispersion.
Write down your 3 carry distances in the notes below.

Transfer to Course
27 May – 2 Jun
Goal: Apply the drills under real scoring pressure. Full 18-hole round mid-week. Tighten wedge circle to 15 ft.

27
Range · 60 min
Pre-Round Simulation
  • Hit shots as if you're on the course — pick specific targets, commit to shape, one shot at a time. No block practice today.
  • Each driver swing: deliberate pre-shot routine. Gate drill feel in the warm-up, then let it go.
  • Every wedge to a specific yardage. No vague "around 120".
